Homemade Fudgesicles - Veggie Desserts What is this? A fudgsicle is a frozen dessert made with chocolate and cream. The chocolate can be either milk or dark, but most commonly you'll find fudgesicles made with milk chocolate. You can find them in nearly any grocery store in the freezer aisle, especially in the summer time when they are most popular.
